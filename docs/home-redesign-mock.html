What it helps with

Simple tools for everyday conversations.

September keeps the things you say often close at hand, so you can spend less energy typing and more energy being understood.

Conversation spaces

Make one space for care, one for family, and one for appointments. Each space keeps the phrases that fit that situation.

One-tap phrases

Save things you say often, like requests, greetings, and preferences. Tap a phrase instead of typing it again.

Speak out loud

Choose a voice and press Speak. You can start with browser voices and connect other voice services later.

How it works

From thought to spoken message.

The message, suggestions, and Speak button stay together, so the next step is always easy to find.

1
Choose a situation Open the space for daily care, family, work, or an appointment.
2
Tap words or phrases Use suggestions to finish the message with fewer taps.
3
Press Speak September reads the message aloud when it is ready.
Setup choices

Choose the setup that feels right.

You can keep everything on this device, use free writing help, or ask a caregiver to connect other services.

Most private Privacy mode

The most private option. No AI service needed.

  • Messages, spaces, and saved phrases stay on this device.
  • Use saved phrases and browser speech.
  • Nothing is sent out for suggestions.
Free start Free AI mode

Use OpenRouter, a free AI option, for writing help.

  • To make suggestions, September may send the current message to OpenRouter.
  • Your spaces and saved phrases still stay on this device.
  • Good when you want help writing longer replies.
Advanced Use your own services

For people or caregivers who already have voice or AI accounts.

  • Add your own Gemini, OpenRouter, or ElevenLabs access key.
  • Choose the voice or writing helper you prefer.
  • September contacts only the services you choose.
