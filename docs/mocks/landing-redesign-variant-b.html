What you can do

Built for full expression with fewer keystrokes

Every feature is designed to save effort without losing your voice.

AI suggestions that learn your style

Context-aware completions drawn from your own message history. Tap a pill instead of typing the whole thing.

Speak in your own voice

Clone your voice from a recording, or choose a professional one. Your words come out sounding like you.

Keep up with conversations

Real-time transcription of what others say, paired with suggested replies so you never lose the thread.

Keyboards for any input

QWERTY, circular, and emoji layouts for mouse, head-tracking, or eye-gaze. Choose what works for your body.
