What it does

It learns how you talk.

September draws on your own message history to suggest what comes next. The more you say, the fewer keys it takes — tap a suggestion instead of spelling it out.

It speaks in your voice.

Clone your own voice or choose a professional one, so the words that leave the screen still sound like you.

It keeps up with the room.

Real-time transcription follows the conversation and offers replies you can send in a tap — so you stay in it, not behind it.

It bends to how you move.

QWERTY, circular, and emoji layouts work with a mouse, head-tracking, or eye-gaze. Large, calm targets, generous spacing, and a pace that waits for you.
